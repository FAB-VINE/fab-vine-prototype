Power Architecture

Do not send LED power through pogo pins.

Each module can draw roughly 2.7A at maximum white brightness. Use thicker 5V/GND wiring and Micro-Fit style plugs for module power. Keep magnetic pogo connectors for light signals, docking, detect lines, or future mechanical alignment.
