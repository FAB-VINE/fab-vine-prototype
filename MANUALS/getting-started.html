Build your first Fab Vine node.

This page is the entry point for new contributors. It will collect the complete first build path: parts, tools, fabrication files, electronics, firmware upload, and testing.

What You Need To Know First

  • Fab Vine nodes are intended to share one unified firmware base.
  • The physical node combines fabricated structure, embedded electronics, OLED expression, sensors, LEDs, and network behavior.
  • Start by understanding the node concept, then move into fabrication, wiring, firmware, and testing.
